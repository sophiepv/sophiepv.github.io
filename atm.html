THE PROGRAM ITSELF

The ATM program is a very rich and dense elective to take. You learn many useful skills and get fast at typing. It is a lot more thorough than any summer camp you can take, hence it lasts three years. It is a commitment. It is full of challenging programs, many of which are used by graphic design professionals, CAD designers, and everyday use. ATM is not just something to get your feet wet with in the world of computer programs, it is more of a knee deep thing. Usually, each program you learn will have at least a month to cover the topic.
